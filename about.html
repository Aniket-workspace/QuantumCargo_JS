Our Competancy

Quantum Cargo Logistics and Traders Private Limited company is a leading logistics company headquartered in Pune-India. Over the years, Quantum Cargo Logistics has pioneered itself as one of the most trusted and reliable end-to-end logistics solutions provider across India and globally.
We provide solutions to more than 125 countries with more than 27+ branch offices across India. Today, Quantum Cargo Logistics offers logistics solutions and seamless connectivity to customers across diverse industry verticals like Fast Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG), Steel, Plastic, Automobile & Auto-components, Projects, Consumer Durable, Pharma, Healthcare, Electrical, Electronics, Textiles, Apparel & Lifestyle, etc.
The experienced and dynamic management team at Quantum Cargo Logistics contributes greatly with new thinking by adopting new technologies, applying latest and modern methods in the field of transportation to help the needs of customers. Quantum Cargo Logistics constantly strives to develop better, more efficient ways to do business, whether it is by implementing cutting-edge technology, increasing the number of services or expanding our network of branches and warehouses. This dynamic mindset is reflected in our motto “Quantum Cargo” and demonstrates a tradition of innovation which is intrinsic to every part of Quantum Cargo Logistics.
